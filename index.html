About Us

A School Built Around the Qur'an

Ibraheem Educational Academy is a Quran memorization school dedicated to helping children build a lifelong bond with the Book of Allah. Our program combines structured Hifz sessions with consistent revision, all within a mosque environment that encourages focus and reverence.

We believe every child has the capacity to memorize the Qur'an when given the right guidance, environment, and support. Our teachers are experienced, patient, and committed to each student's progress.

Our Mission

To raise a generation of Huffaz who understand, love, and live by the Qur'an from a young age.

Our Vision

To be the leading Hifz program in Ikorodu, known for producing confident and consistent memorizers.
